In the Roots & Streams interactive, users are able to discover how artists influence one another across genres, learn something new about familiar artists, and create a playlist of known and new artists to listen to after the visit.

Process

Discover

Who is the audience?

Local visitors / School Field Trips

Travelers with all ages / Music Lovers

Experience Goals

1. Make Connections: See how artists influence one another, across genres, allowing musical styles to both adapt and endure.

2. Discover: Discover new artists, songs, and genres starting from a familiar starting point.

3. Surprise: Learn something new about familiar artists.

4. Self-Exploration: Experience the database in a free-flow exploration.
