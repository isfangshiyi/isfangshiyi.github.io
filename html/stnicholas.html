The St. Nicholas National Shrine is a Greek Orthodox Church next to the 9/11 Memorial in NYC. The original church was destroyed on 9/11 and is now being rebuilt. It will open to the public in the spring of 2022.

Our team is developing an immersive audio tour that provides 360° view exploration, virtual light, and candle and multi-language options for global visitors.
